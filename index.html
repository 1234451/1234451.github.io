《Dream》

作品：《梦境》

 Author: Wang Maoxiang                                                                作者：王茂湘

Instructor: Ziv Zeev Cohen 、 Li Xinyi                                       指导老师：Ziv Zeev Cohen   、  李心夷

Course: Lost Light-Multimedia Sensory Experience             课程：光迹迷航——多媒体感官体验

Work form: film, installation                                                           作品形式：影片、装置

ntroduction:Combined with the visual effect of overlapping stairs, this story is presented from the perspective of the first person or a voyeur. The work itself is an interactive device, and in the later stage, the peeping perspective is presented by means of film and television. Dream is a derivative of emotion, with a strong personal color, and dreams form a special world. It is difficult for us to distinguish between the real world and the unreal world, even in the real world. Similarly, dreams don't need language, and the whole process is silent. In order to better present the idea, there are only pictures and breathing heartbeat in the short film. In the middle of the film, all sounds will disappear. From the time of existence to non-existence, the viewer will have false tinnitus and vertigo, which will make the viewer feel bored or irritable, and at the same time extend the time and finally end the experience.

作品介绍：结合楼梯不断重叠的视觉效果，以第一人称或者说是一个偷窥者的视角去展现这个故事。作品本身是互动装置，后期采用影视的方式呈现偷窥视角。梦是情的派生物，带有强烈的个人色彩，入梦形成特殊的世界。我们很难去分辨什么是真实的世界，什么是虚幻的世界，即使在现实世界中，也很难区分。同样梦不需要语言，整个过程悄无声息，为了更好的呈现理念短片当中仅有画面和呼吸心跳声。影片中期所有的声音将会消失，从有到无这个过程，观看者会出现虚假的耳鸣和眩晕感，不真实感，使观看者产生无聊亦或者是烦躁的情绪，同时延伸时间，最终结束的体验。

Works concept:
To make such a work, the most important thing is to reflect all my dreams. Most of the time, we are not interested in other people's stories. We don't include some earth-shattering people here. What I'm talking about is the people around us everyday. Of course, this may only be my personal feelings, and what may not be interested is your nonsense. There is a paragraph in the film where there is no sound and the picture I see is slow. This is what I want to create a boring feeling. The original intention of this work is to show people my description of dreams in a more direct and interesting way. I don't think many people are willing to listen to you tell a long story about disorderly dreams. Our descriptions of dreams are often very general, such as "scary dreams", "interesting dreams", "scary dreams", "happy dreams" and "strange dreams". I often dream, but I don't think it's a bad thing. In dreams, there are many things missing, such as weak sense of smell (in fact, there is no sense of smell at all, and the taste in dreams is created by memory), slow movement and other maddening scenes, and I may not remember anything when I wake up the next day. Dreams also create wonderful things, and this is what I want to show you. To me, this feeling is an illusion, but it makes me look forward to it.

作品理念：
制作这样一个作品，最主要的是想体现自身做梦所有的体会。很多时候我们对别人的故事并不感兴趣，这里不包括一些惊天动地的人物，我所说的都是日常身边的人。当然这或许这只是我个人的感受，可能不感兴趣的只是你的废话。影片中有一段，没有声音，看到的画面动作迟缓，这就是我想要营造出的无聊至极的感受。这个作品的初衷就是想以更直接，更有意思的方式直观的给人们展示我对梦的描绘。我想没有多少人愿意听你诉说一长段关于杂乱无序的梦，我们对梦的描述往往非常概括像“恐怖的梦” “有意思的梦” “吓人的梦” “幸福的梦” “奇怪的梦”这一类。我时常做梦，但并不觉得这是一件坏事。梦中，会缺少很多东西，比如嗅觉弱化（啊其实根本没有啦，梦中的味道也是凭借记忆制造出来的），动作缓慢等等让人抓狂的场景，第二天醒来或许什么也不记得了。梦中也会创造
出奇妙无比的内容，而这就是我想给大家所呈现的东西。对我来说这种感觉就是一种幻觉，却又让我无比期待。

Creative process

创作过程

Initial stage    初期

Being in a space of dream and reality creates an illusory atmosphere. The space of dream and reality that I want to make is a description of the picture in my mind. Being in this space will make me feel relaxed and comfortable.
Such a space will make me feel separated from real life (temporarily), but it won't make me feel alienated.
处于梦与现实的一个空间，营造出虚幻的氛围。想要做出来的这个梦与现实的空间，是我对自己脑海里画面的一个描述。处于这个空间会让我觉得很放松、舒适。
这样的一个空间会让我觉得脱离了现实生活（暂时），却不会让我产生疏离感。

 Metaphase 中期

Stacked stairs are the process of entering dreams.
There are two ways here:
1. Take the stairs. Sometimes insomnia may last for an hour, two hours or even longer. This process is very boring and even painful. Just like this staircase, you don't know how many floors it has, and you don't know when it will go to the end. It is very boring and painful (every time you suffer from insomnia, you will think, "When can I fall asleep? I really want to sleep." At this time, "When can I go to the end?").
2. Fall from the middle to the bottom. Sometimes, when sleeping, the body will feel falling, like being shot with a 0.5-fold falling shot. Until the bottom, to dream.
1.走楼梯。有时失眠可能会持续一个小时、两个小时甚至更长时间。这个过程很无聊，甚至很痛苦。就像这个楼梯。你不知道它有几层，也不知道它什么时候会结束。这很无聊也很痛苦(每次你失眠的时候，你都会想，“什么时候能睡着？我真的很想睡觉。”那重复的楼梯就是“我什么时候能走到尽头？”)

Final presentation呈现

In the later stage, I combined the previous two ideas, combined with projector, and made it with pr, presenting the space of the dream process from the first perspective.
后期我把前面两者想法结合在一起，结合投影仪的方式用pr制作，以第一视角呈现这个梦境过程的空间。
